Here is a time line of Michael Jackson's life

1958: Born on 29 August in Gary, Indiana.

1964: Joined the Jackson Brothers with Marlon, a band formed by their father which included brothers Jackie, and later the the name group was changed in Jackson 5

1968: The Jackson 5 recorded the first single 'Big Boy'

1970: Began to emerge as a solo performer

1975: The Jackson 5 signed with Epic Records and renamed themselves the Jacksons

1978: Jackson's work in film began in 'The Wiz'

1980: Won 3 awards at the American Music Awards for his solo efforts: Favorite Soul/R&B Album, Favorite Soul/R&B Male Artist, and Favorite Soul/R&B Single for "Don't Stop 'Til You Get Enough"

1981-1983: Recorded with Freddie Mercury

1982: Released the album 'Thriller' and won 7 grammy

1983: 'Thriller' became the best-selling album worldwide also became the best-selling album of all time in the United States and Worldwide

1992: Founded the Heal the world foundation

1993: Jackson was accused of child sexual abuse by a 13-year-old boy and also he was addicted to the drugs

1994-1996: Jackson married Lisa Marie Presley

1996-1999: Jackson married Debbie Rowe

2009: Died at 50 years in Los Angeles
